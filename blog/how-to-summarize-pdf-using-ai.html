Tips for Getting the Best AI Summaries

📄 Works best with text-heavy PDFs

SwiftMind™ excels with PDFs that contain actual selectable text — reports, research papers, legal documents, textbooks, articles, and policy documents. For scanned or image-based PDFs where text can't be selected, use TextLens™ OCR first to extract the text, then summarize.

🌐 Choose the right summary language

If you're an English speaker reading a Hindi document, select "English" as the summary language — the AI will summarize the Hindi content in English for you. If you prefer the summary in the same language as the document, select "Auto-detect."

📑 Best results with 5–50 page documents

SwiftMind™ reads up to 30 pages for context. For very long documents (100+ pages), the AI summarizes the most information-dense sections it can access. For the most accurate summaries of very long documents, consider using SwiftMind™ on specific chapters or sections rather than the entire document.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many pages can SwiftMind™ summarize?

SwiftMind™ reads up to 30 pages of your PDF for context. For most practical documents — research papers, contracts, reports, and chapters — this covers everything. For very long documents, you'll get a summary of the most accessible content.

Does SwiftMind™ work with scanned PDFs?

SwiftMind™ works with PDFs that contain actual selectable text. If your PDF is a scanned image (where you can't highlight text), use TextLens™ OCR first to extract the text, then paste it into a document to summarize.

Can I get the summary in Hindi?

Yes — select "Hindi" as the summary language before clicking Summarize. The AI will deliver the full structured summary in Hindi, even if the source document is in English.

Is my PDF kept private?

Yes. Your PDF file never leaves your browser. SwiftMind™ extracts the text locally on your device, then only the text (not the file) is sent to the AI for summarization. No file is stored on any server.

How accurate is the AI summary?

SwiftMind™ uses Gemini 2.5 Flash — one of the most capable AI models available — which produces highly accurate, context-aware summaries. However, like all AI tools, it works best with well-structured, clear documents. Extremely technical or highly specialized jargon may occasionally be simplified beyond the intended meaning.

Can I use SwiftMind™ and DocuTalk™ together?

Yes — this is actually the recommended workflow. Use SwiftMind™ first to get an overview of the document, then switch to DocuTalk™ Chat to ask specific questions about sections you want to explore in more depth.
