How to Extract Specific Pages from a Large PDF for Free

Imagine you have a massive 200-page ebook or a heavy financial statement, but you only need to share pages 15 to 18 with a client. Sending the entire heavy file looks unprofessional and compromises your data privacy.

The easiest solution is to split the document and extract only the relevant pages. Here is a quick tutorial on how to separate and extract specific pages from a PDF file for free without installing heavy plugins.

Top Use Cases for Splitting PDFs

  • Sharing Selective Data: Send only the specific charts or chapters your team needs.
  • Reducing Storage Limits: Remove useless pages or blank sheets to drastically reduce your file size.
  • Extracting Certificates: Separate individual certificates from a bulk compiled legal or academic file.
