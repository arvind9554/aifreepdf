What is "Chat with PDF" and How Does It Work?

"Chat with PDF" is an AI feature that lets you have a natural conversation with the content of a document. Instead of using Ctrl+F to search for keywords, or scrolling through dozens of pages to find what you need, you simply ask a question in plain language — and the AI reads the document and gives you a direct answer.

Here's what happens behind the scenes when you use DocuTalk™:

  1. Text extraction — Your PDF's text content is extracted directly in your browser using pdf.js. No upload to any server.
  2. Context building — The document content becomes the AI's "knowledge base" for your conversation.
  3. Question processing — Your question is sent to the AI along with the document context via a secure API call.
  4. Answer generation — The AI reads the relevant sections and gives you a precise, context-aware answer.
  5. Follow-up conversation — The AI remembers your previous questions, so you can ask follow-up questions naturally.

🔒 Privacy first: Your PDF file itself never leaves your device. Only the extracted text content is sent to the AI API when you ask a question — and only for that question. Nothing is stored permanently.

Tips for Getting the Best Answers

🎯 Be specific in your questions

Instead of asking "Tell me about this document", ask "What is the payment schedule mentioned in this contract?" or "What does Section 4 say about warranties?" Specific questions get specific answers.

🔄 Use follow-up questions

DocuTalk™ remembers your previous questions in the same session. So after asking "What are the key findings?", you can follow up with "Can you explain the third finding in simpler terms?" without repeating context.

🌐 Ask in your own language

You can ask questions in English or Hindi (or most other languages). If the document is in English and you ask in Hindi, the AI will respond in Hindi — making it genuinely accessible for everyone.

📄 Best for text-heavy PDFs

DocuTalk™ works best with PDFs that contain actual text — reports, contracts, textbooks, articles, research papers, and legal documents. For scanned or image-based PDFs, use TextLens™ OCR first to extract the text, then chat with the result.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does DocuTalk™ work with scanned PDFs?

DocuTalk™ works with PDFs that contain actual selectable text. If your PDF is a scanned image (like a photographed document), the text won't be extractable directly. In that case, use TextLens™ OCR first to extract and copy the text, then paste it into a new document or use the summarizer.

How many pages can DocuTalk™ read?

DocuTalk™ currently reads up to 25 pages of your PDF for context. For most documents — contracts, reports, chapters, research papers — this covers everything. For very long documents, start with the most relevant section.

Is my document private when using DocuTalk™?

Yes. Your PDF file itself never leaves your browser. Only the extracted text content is sent to the AI when you ask a question — and nothing is permanently stored. When you close the tab, all data is gone.

Can I ask questions in Hindi?

Yes — you can ask questions in Hindi (or most other languages) and receive answers in that language. The AI is multilingual and understands context regardless of the language you use.

Does it remember what I asked before?

Yes, within the same session. DocuTalk™ maintains conversation history so you can ask follow-up questions naturally. If you close the tab and come back, the conversation starts fresh.
