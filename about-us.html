About AIFreePDF

A free, private, browser-based PDF platform built for students, professionals, and everyone who works with documents.

✅
Our promise: Every tool on AIFreePDF is 100% free, requires no signup, adds no watermarks, and processes your files entirely in your browser — your documents never leave your device.

Who We Are

AIFreePDF is a free online platform offering professional-grade PDF and image processing tools — all running directly in your browser. We built this because we believe powerful document tools should be accessible to everyone, not locked behind expensive subscriptions or intrusive sign-up walls.

Whether you're a student compressing an assignment before submission, a professional merging reports for a client, or someone who just needs to split a scanned document — AIFreePDF is designed to get the job done in seconds, with zero friction.

Our Story

AIFreePDF was founded by Arvind Kumar Pandey, an independent developer passionate about building tools that solve real everyday problems. The idea was simple: most PDF tools online are either too expensive, too cluttered with ads, or require you to upload sensitive files to unknown servers.

The solution was to build a platform that processes everything locally in the user's browser — using modern web technologies like pdf.js and pdf-lib — so there is literally no server to upload to, no data to collect, and no privacy risk at all.

What started as a simple PDF compressor has grown into a full suite of tools including AI-powered features like PDF summarization, document chat, and OCR text extraction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to professional document tools. We believe:

  • Free means truly free. No hidden limits, no "free trial" countdowns, no watermarks on your output, no credit card required.
  • Speed matters. Every tool should work instantly — no waiting for server processing, no queue, no loading spinners for 30 seconds.
  • Simplicity is powerful. The best tool is the one that does exactly what you need without making you figure out 15 settings first.

What We Offer

AIFreePDF provides two categories of tools:

  • Standard PDF Tools: PDF Compressor, Image to PDF, Merge PDF, Split PDF, Rotate PDF, PDF to Images, Reorder Pages, Protect PDF, PDF Editor, and Image Quality Enhancer — all 100% browser-based with zero server involvement.
  • AI-Powered Tools: SwiftMind™ PDF Summarizer, DocuTalk™ Chat with PDF, and TextLens™ OCR — powered by Google Gemini AI for intelligent document processing.

Technology

AIFreePDF is built with a philosophy of minimal dependencies and maximum privacy:

  • pdf.js — Open-source PDF rendering engine from Mozilla
  • pdf-lib — JavaScript library for creating and modifying PDF files
  • Tesseract.js — Browser-based OCR engine (no server needed)
  • Google Gemini API — AI processing for summarization, chat, and text polish (text only, never the file)
  • Vercel — Hosting platform for fast global delivery
